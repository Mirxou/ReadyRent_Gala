# Sovereign Constitution

**Version:** 1.0 (Preparatory)  
**Date:** February 7, 2026  
**Status:** 📜 **Master Document**

---

## Preamble

We build not an app, but a scale. The purpose of this system is to establish a platform where justice is accessible, ownership is respected, and the human context is never sacrificed for algorithmic efficiency. This Constitution is the highest law of the system, governing its development, its judges, and its users.

---

## Article I: Principles of Justice (العدالة)

Justice in this system is absolute and non-negotiable.

1. **No Judgment Without Evidence:** No decision shall be rendered without established proof. The absence of evidence is not an invitation to assume; it is a mandate to pause.
2. **Burden of Proof:** The claimant bears the burden of establishing their case. The default state is innocence/parity until proven otherwise.
3. **The Interpretation of Doubt:** In cases of genuine ambiguity where evidence is equally weighted or missing, the system shall interpret doubt in favor of peace and reconciliation rather than conflict expansion.
4. **Finality through Human Review:** While AI may assist, organize, and suggest, no final judgment that impacts human property or reputation shall be executed without conscious human validation.

---

## Article II: Stewardship & Accountability (الاستخلاف والمسؤولية)

The managers and developers of this system are its stewards, not its owners.

1. **System Service:** The technology exists to serve the users. Any update that prioritize system growth over user fairness is a violation of this Constitution.
2. **Judge Accountability:** Every individual empowered to act as a judge within this system is accountable for their decisions. Transparency must be maintained without violating the privacy of the parties involved.
3. **No Oppression:** The system shall never be used as a tool for systemic bias or the targeting of specific groups. Its rules must be applied universally and blind to external status.

---

## Article III: Human Dignity & Mercy

Efficiency is a secondary goal; Justice and Mercy are primary.

1. **Context over Speed:** A fast judgment that ignores the human context of a dispute is a failed judgment.
2. **Mercy as a Variable:** The system must allow for settlement and mercy (صلح). If both parties agree to a solution that bypasses rigid rules, the system must facilitate it.
3. **Respect for Privacy:** Data is a trust. Anonymization is not just a feature; it is an ethical obligation to protect the reputation of individuals while maintaining the public's right to transparency.

---

## Article IV: Future Sovereignty

1. **Readiness for the Decentralized Era:** This system shall be designed to eventually exist beyond its central creators. Its logic should be clear enough to be translated into decentralized protocols (Web3) when the time is right.
2. **Unchangeable Core:** These Articles are the "Silent Roots." They may be expanded, but their core intent—Justice over Efficiency—shall not be repealed.
